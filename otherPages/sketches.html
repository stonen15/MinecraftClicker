Sketches

Start Screen Sketch

The start screen will provide the player with the game's premise, information on how the game is played, and the play button.

Main Screen Sketch

This will be the main page/hub of our game. This is where most of the activity and player interaction takes place.

Flower: This is the where the icon will be placed. The icon will be clickable by the player. Once the icon is clicked, the score will increase.

Score (money count): This is represented by the numbers on the top of the page. As the player click the icon, the score will go up.

Shop: Opens the "Shops" menu.

Help: Opens the "Help" bar.

Settings: Opens the "Settings" menu.

Shop Screen Sketch

This is the shop menu. This is where the player can spend their money to purchase items.

Item: A varity of items can be purchased in the shop. These items can range from multi-clicks to auto-clicks. The main point of items is to assist the player to achieve a higher score.

Description: The description of the item, along with its effect/function, is described in the box.

Help Screen Sketch

This screen will provide the player the "Game Premise" and "How to Play" information.

Setting Screen Sketch

This screen shows the settings menu. In the settings menu, the player is able to turn on sound effects and music.
