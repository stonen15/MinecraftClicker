Help

Start Screen

This is the start screen. In the label, you can enter your name which will be displayed on the top left corner of the screen.

Game Screens

These are the screens where the main game take place. The game starts on the first screen with the wood block. As you play the game, you will hit milestones where the the other blocks and background screens will be unlocked. Each block has a different value, with diamond block being being highest.

Shop Popup

This is the shop window where items are available for purchase. Items provide a variety of effects and the price for each one is located to the left of each item. The item can be purchased by clicking its image.

Items

These are the items available for purchase in the game. As your money exceeds the price of the item, the item and description will light up. To purchase, you must click the image.

There are 4 types of items: redstone, axe, pickaxe, and special.
As you purchase an item, the item will be replaced by an upgraded item.
Each item has a different function.

Settings

This is the settings page. Here settings such as ingame music volume can be controlled to provide a more comfortable gaming experiance.

The Krnic cheat button will give the player a large amount of money. It is given to Mr. Krnic only.

The End

This is the boss fight sequence of the game. The player will face off against the Ender dragon by clicking it until the dragons health points reach zero.

Clock

The clock will display the amount of seconds that have passed since the boss sequence began. After the boss sequence concludes the time will be recorded.

Health Bar

This is where the Ender Dragons health points will be displayed. Every click will lower the health points by 1.

Time Record

This is where the best time will be display after you beat The End. The time displayed in the end after you beat the Ender Dragon will be put into a varible that will be bubble sorted to display the best time or the lowest time.
